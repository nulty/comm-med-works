Community Mediation Works

Community Mediation Works provide a free and confidential conflict resolution service for low-income communities throughout Dún Laoghaire Rathdown and in Bray. Through support, facilitation and mediation the concerned parties can address the issues surrounding a problem and develop solutions that meet the needs of all involved.

Community Mediation can be used to effectively address a range of issues including, but not limited to:

  1. Vandalism / Anti-social behaviour
  2. Threats and intimidation
  3. Property maintenence
  4. Family issues
  5. Landlord and tenant disputes
  6. Environmental issues
  7. Youth related issues
  8. Harassment

Our voluntary mediators can meet you at a time and place of yourconvenience to discuss the issues concerning you, explore your options and help you decide if community mediation is the appropriate route for you take.
